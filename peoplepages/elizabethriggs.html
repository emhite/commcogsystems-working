Elizabeth Riggs

Graduate student


My research interests focus on the cognitive processing of narratives, with a focus on audio and audiovisual narratives. In particular, I am interested in studying the role of attention during engagement with media. Currently, I am collaborating with Dr. Coronel and Dr. Krajbich on a project which studies the role of attention in decision-making for political ballot measures.
